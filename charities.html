Massachusetts Department of Children and Families was founded because people in massachusetts wanted to help children who have been through hard times. DCF's 30+ locations in Massachusetts all have the same mission, helping every child find a home. DCF is a charity that helps abused and/or fostered children, find a home. DCF tries to find a home for every child through adoption help and more. DCF has 30+ locations all located across Massachusetts. You can donate money, books, clothes, toys, and more in order to support the kids that rely on DCF. Live Love Give reccomends this charity because DCF helps so many children each year. One of our team members even has adopted siblings through DCF and would highly reccomend their friendly and impacting services.

Humane Society International was founded in 2001 to help animals who were stray/struggling. They are one of the only international humane societies in the world. Help animals by encouraging going vegan, cage free animals, and very against getting rid of animals in streets, harmful shelters, and animal abuse. You can donate or be a volunteer to help animals in need. We recommend this charity because we believe that animals are living and deserve to be treated decently.

Doctors Without Borders or Médecins Sans Frontières, started in May 1968, when a group of young doctors wanted to help during conflict in Paris. DWB provides healthcare to people who do not have access to hospitals or basic medical care. DWB helps people in war zones, after natural disasters, and during epidemics. You can donate money, you can volunteer, or you can fundraise for Doctors without Borders. Live Love Give recommends this organization because they provide care to thousands of people all over the world, and they change the lives of people without access to basic medical care.
