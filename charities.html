Charities

Dana Farber Cancer Institute was founded in 1947 in Boston, Massachusetts. Dana-Farber Cancer Institute is an organization working to defeat cancer. They provide children and adults with the best available cancer treatment and care, and research the newest technology to develop cures. You can help by contributing money to support cancer research and treatment, volunteering, or donating. We reccomend this charity because it helps a lot of people and has many awards including the Facility of the Year Award, a TurnKey Award from ALN Magazine, and a gold award by the New Hampshire Union Leader’s annual Readers’ Choice program.

Boston Cancer Research Foundation has a strong passion for helping people in need. They were voted the best Breast Cancer Organization and are donating billions of dollars each year in order to help this cause. BCRF helps people with Breast Cancer by donating to this cause, researching this disease, and trying to find a cure for this tragic disease. Users can donate money to help people with Breast Cancer and they can hold fundraisers in order to help this powerful organization. Live Love Give would recommend this organization because it was voted the best Breast Cancer Organization in the world and is eager to help others in need.

Marisa Tufaro Foundation. Marisa Tufaro was born with a complex cardiac defect. When she tragically passed away at age 13 due to heart transplant complications, her family started a foundation to give back to Boston Children's, Philadelphia Children's, and many other places, to help children in need. MTF gives money for scholarships, to several hospitals, and many other organizations. Their main goal is to provide young medical patients with things that they want/need. You can donate and/or give gift cards or other gifts to help children. We recommend this charity because it is very important to give back, and one of our founder's families knew her personally.

Greater Boston Food Bank , which is located in Boston, Massachusetts was founded in 1981. The idea was inspired by events in the Great Depression when Kip's grandmother started distributing food during this dark age. Then in the 1970's and 1980's, Kip started distributing food in her station wagon to hungry people, and that is how GBFB started. GBFB donates food to people who can't afford it and helps people get back on their feet and keep them happy and healthy. Users can donate to GBFB and help every person and family, put food on their table. GBFB will get food for these people and change their lives for the better. Live Love Give recommends this organization because they help so many families/people each year fight hunger. They are also the largest food bank in New England and one of the largest food banks in the country.

Perkins School for the Blind is a famous school that helps blind children realize their potential. Some famous alumni are Helen Keller and Laura Bridgman, the first deafblind person to read. Perkins educates, trains and improves the lives of approximately 4.5 million children around the world, and provides multiple services and training programs for deaf or blind people. They also have a library with 530,000 items in braille, audio, electronic and large print formats that provide deafblind people with ways to read. You can donate to give Perkins money to provide more services and help more people, and you can volunteer at Perkins. We recommend this school because it is the oldest school for the blind in the US, and has helped many deafblind people become successful.

American Red Cross helps people recovering from world and health diasters all across America. American Red Cross is a non profit organization that was founded by Clara Barton in order to help soldiers in the civil war. Overtime, this organization spread and started helping out people in or recovering from disasters all over the world. Clara was a nurse and a very powerful woman figure in a time when women weren't as powerful, and this sends a really good message about how women can start a powerful and successful organization. You can donate money in order to help with this organization or you can volenteer and help America along the 500,000 other volenteers that help with the many diasters each year. Live Love Give reccomends this charity because this organization helps so many people and are passionate about helping people all across America. Founded for an important and unforgetting purpose, the American Red Cross shows America that helping is always the right answer.

Cradles to Crayons was founded in 2002 by Lynn Margherio, after she noticed how many outgrown or even unworn clothes her niece had. Cradles to Crayons is a charity that gives essentials to disadvantaged children, that kids shouldn't have to go without. Cradles to Crayons has offices in Chicago, Boston and Philadelphia. They donate new clothes and new toys to kids in need. You can donate money, new clothes, new toys and books, and your time by volunteering at their warehouses. Live Love Give recommends this organization because they serve over 200,000 children every year, and we believe they have a big impact on our community, and many others.
