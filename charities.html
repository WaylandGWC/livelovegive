Live Love Give's March Spotlight

Each month Live Love Give chooses a charities to be in the spotlight. This is a charity that has done a lot recently to help and we want everyone to know. This month's charity is Cradles to Crayons.

The Marisa Tufaro Foundation Marisa Tufaro was born with a complex cardiac defect. When she tragically passed away at age 13 due to heart transplant complications, her family started a foundation to give back to Boston Children's, Philadelphia Children's, and many other places. to help children in need. MTF gives money for scholarships, to several hospitals, and many other organizations.
