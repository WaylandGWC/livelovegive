Cancer Charities

Dana Farber Cancer Institute was founded in 1947 in Boston, Massachusetts. Dana-Farber Cancer Institute is an organization working to defeat cancer. They provide children and adults with the best available cancer treatment and care, and research the newest technology to develop cures. You can help by contributing money to support cancer research and treatment, volunteering, or donating. We reccomend this charity because it helps a lot of people and has many awards including the Facility of the Year Award, a TurnKey Award from ALN Magazine, and a gold award by the New Hampshire Union Leader’s annual Readers’ Choice program.

Breast Cancer Research Foundation has a strong passion for helping people in need. They were voted the best breast cancer organization in the world, and are donating billions of dollars each year in order to help this cause. BCRF helps people with breast cancer by donating to this cause, researching this disease, and trying to find a cure for this tragic disease. Users can donate money to help people with breast cancer and they can hold fundraisers in order to help this powerful organization. Live Love Give would recommend this organization because it is reccomended by many people, and is eager to help others in need.

Massachusetts General Hospital Cancer Center was founded to treat and fight cancer in 1986 by Kurt Isselbacher MD. The MGH Cancer Center is a section of the Massachusetts General Hospital that treats cancer and does lots of research to find a cure for cancer. They provide customized, innovative treatments to adults and children. You can donate money or do a fundraiser. We are supportive of MGH because one of our team members has lost loved ones due to cancer and we want to give back.

American Cancer Society is a non profit orginization that's goal is to eliminate cancer for good. Founded in 1913, for over 100 years, The American Cancer Society has been helping many people all across the world with this terrible disease. You can donate money online or help them by funraising for their increadible orginization. We reccomend this orginization because they were rated one of the top 10 non profit cancer orginizations in the world and their mission inspires all of us to give to those in need, which is one of our main philosophies here at Live Love Give.
