Marisa Tufaro Foundation. Marisa Tufaro was born with a complex cardiac defect. When she tragically passed away at age 13 due to heart transplant complications, her family started a foundation to give back to Boston Children's, Philadelphia Children's, and many other places, to help children in need. MTF gives money for scholarships, to several hospitals, and many other organizations. Their main goal is to provide young medical patients with things that they want/need. You can donate and/or give gift cards or other gifts to help children. We recommend this charity because it is very important to give back, and one of our founder's families knew her personally.

Massachusetts Department of Children and Families was founded because people in massachusetts wanted to help children who have been through hard times. DCF's 30+ locations in Massachusetts all have the same mission, helping every child find a home. DCF is a charity that helps abused and/or fostered children, find a home. DCF tries to find a home for every child through adoption help and more. DCF has 30+ locations all located across Massachusetts. You can donate money, books, clothes, toys, and more in order to support the kids that rely on DCF. Live Love Give reccomends this charity because DCF helps so many children each year. One of our team members even has adopted siblings through DCF and would highly reccomend their friendly and impacting services.

Cradles to Crayons was founded in 2002 by Lynn Margherio, after she noticed how many outgrown or even unworn clothes her niece had. Cradles to Crayons is a charity that gives essentials to disadvantaged children, that kids shouldn't have to go without. Cradles to Crayons has offices in Chicago, Boston and Philadelphia. They donate new clothes and new toys to kids in need. You can donate money, new clothes, new toys and books, and your time by volunteering at their warehouses. Live Love Give recommends this organization because they serve over 200,000 children every year, and we believe they have a big impact on our community, and many others.
