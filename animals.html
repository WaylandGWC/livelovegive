Humane Society International was founded in 2001 to help animals who were stray/struggling. They are one of the only international humane societies in the world. Help animals by encouraging going vegan, cage free animals, and very against getting rid of animals in streets, harmful shelters, and animal abuse. You can donate or be a volunteer to help animals in need. We recommend this charity because we believe that animals are living and deserve to be treated decently.

ASPCA was founded on the belief that all animals are entitled to kind and respectful treatment for everyone. They were founded in 1866. The american society for the prevention of cruelty to animals was one of the first charities to help animals and are now one of the most well known worldwide. The headquarters of ASPCA is New York City. They have volunteers and employees who help heal hurt animals, feed starving animals, and prevent any sort of cruelty. They are proud of their 2 million supporters all over the globe. Become one today! You can donate, volunteer, and adopt an animal to help them out. We are really all for the support of helping animals. we believe that every animal deserves a safe, loving family and should never be harmed in any way.
