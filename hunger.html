Greater Boston Food Bank , which is located in Boston, Massachusetts was founded in 1981 by Kip Tiernan. The idea was inspired by events in the Great Depression when Kip's grandmother started distributing food to the hungry. Then in the 1970's and 1980's, Tiernan started distributing food in her station wagon to hungry people, and that is how GBFB started. GBFB donates food to people who can't afford it and helps people get back on their feet and keep them happy and healthy. Users can donate to GBFB and help every person and family, and put food on their table. GBFB will get food for these people in need and change their lives for the better. Live Love Give recommends this organization because they help so many families/people each year fight hunger. They are also the largest food bank in New England and one of the largest food banks in the country.

Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation , was founded by Bill and Melinda Gates in 2000, when it merged with the Gates Learning Foundation. The Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation works to help all people lead healthy, productive lives, especially people in extreme poverty, hunger and living in developing countries. They work with partner orginazations around the world to help people in need. Some issues they are tackling right now are supporting vulnerable children and families in Washington and hunger in developing countries. You can donate so that the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation can help even more people. We reccomend this organization because they change the lives of people all over the world and they are a very reliable charity to donate to.

Action Against Hunger , Action against hunger was founded in 1979 and helps people who face challenges, that lead to hunger. They help millions of people worldwide evry year and have never stopped doing their amazing work. The Action against Hunger organization is passionate about elping those going through life difficulties, which makes getting food challenging. When you donate to Action Against Hunger, you are helping this organization save people all over the world from one of the worst things imaginable, hunger. We reccomend this organization because Action Against Hunger has already helped over 14.7 million people already, and yet there is still hunger in our world. Donate or volunteer today and help defeat hunger, together.
